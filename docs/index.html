Setters Scheduler

Schedule PDF to Google Calendar, in one click.

Setters Scheduler is a Chrome extension that reads a volleyball league or tournament schedule PDF open in your browser tab, finds your team's games, and adds them to your Google Calendar.

Get it on the Chrome Web Store

Features

  • Parses the schedule PDF directly in your browser tab — no upload, no external service
  • Supports weekly league schedules and single-night tournament brackets
  • Pick your team and which Google Calendar the games go on
  • Skips games already on the calendar, so re-running it doesn't create duplicates
